Our Values: S.P.A.C.E.

REALMS has five core values that support our students and staff:

Safety - All children will learn in our safe and supportive environment

Partnerships - We believe education is a partnership amongst students, educators, parents, and the community

Accountability - Children at REALMS learn that life is about choices, personal responsibility, and personal accountability. Also, adults at REALMS are held to high standards of professionalism and are held accountable to them

Communication - We believe that healthy communication is the foundation for relationships, progress, and success in school and beyond

Equality - At REALMS, we welcome all students and families and act in such a way that every person is treated with dignity and respect
