Charter schools are not allowed to discourage a pupil from enrolling or seeking to enroll in the charter school because the pupil exhibits any characteristics such as students with disabilities, academically low-achieving, English learners, neglected or delinquent, homeless, socially economically disadvantaged, foster youth, or based on nationality, race, ethnicity, or sexual orientation. Charter schools are not allowed to request a pupil's records before enrollment or encourage a child who is enrolled in a charter school to disenroll or transfer to another school. A parent, guardian, or pupil (18 years or older) may file a Charter School Complaint Form to the authorizing entity if they suspect the charter school is in violation of Education Code Section 47605(e)(4).

Returning Students
  1. Visit: Aeries for returning students and log into your existing parent account
  2. A yellow banner will appear and stay up until the re-enrollment process is completed.
  3. Click the "click here" link. It will then direct you to the re-enrollment page.
  4. Read and follow the instructions, adding or making changes as needed.
  5. The re-enrollment page will have 4 steps to complete: Family Information, Student, Contact, and Final Data Confirmation.
  6. At the end, click on the CONFIRM and CONTINUE button.
  7. You will then see your child's emergency card. Add or make changes as needed. You must print and sign the emergency card and turn it in on the first day of school.
New Students
  1. Visit Aeries for new students to create a new Aeries account for enrolling your students*
  2. Click the "Enroll A New Student" button in the lower right.
  3. Select the desired school year and click "Next".
  4. Verify you have the required information available and press "Next".
  5. Create a new account.
  6. You will receive a confirmation email.
  7. Follow the instructions in Aeries to finish enrolling your students.
*Please note: New enrollment requires creating a new Aeries account. This new account is different than a previous Aeries account you may have used at a different school.
