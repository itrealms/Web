REALMS Summer Camp Sign-Ups

Hello students, parents & guardians!

We are excited to share our Summer Camp 2024 schedule with you. This year we are adding two new exciting classes; ROBOTICS and MODEL ROCKETRY. This will give opportunities to apply many of the skills that students have been learning at REALMS as well as a chance to explore creative approaches to other fields thru engaging exercises, puzzles and projects! Please take some time to read each of the class descriptions below to learn about some of the fun and captivating ways that students can maintain what they have worked hard to learn this school year and develop new skills and interests as well.

Free breakfast, lunch, and a snack will be provided.

Camp will be help 5 days a week Mon-Fri and meals offered every day.
