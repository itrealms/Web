REALMS Summer Camp Sign-Ups

Hello students, parents & guardians!

We are excited to share our Summer Camp 2024 schedule with you. This year the focus of summer camp will be ROBOTICS! This will give opportunities to apply many of the skills that students have been learning at REALMS. We will be exploring a variety of avenues for applying ROBOTICS, reading, writing, math, science, Spanish, music, and art skills through creative projects and activities. Please take some time to read each of the class descriptions below to learn about some of the fun and engaging ways that students can maintain over the summer what they have worked hard to learn this school year.

Free breakfast and lunch will be provided at the times below:

Breakfast: M, T, W, Th, F - 7:30am to 8:00am
Lunch: M, T, W, Th, F - 11:15am to 12:00pm

Please plan to drop students off no earlier than 7:30am since no supervision will be provided prior to 7:30am. The camp will end at 4:30pm, so please plan to pick students up promptly at 4:30pm.

Summer Camp will run from June 5 to July 19. With breaks on June 19, July 4 and July 5. No camps these days.

You will need to sign-up each child for each block you wish to attend. Please remember to sign up for the correct session(s) on the form. We hope that students will enjoy this Summer Camp as much as we will!
